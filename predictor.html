About

This page is about figuring out a player's future stats given factors like their age. Not meant to be taken seriously.

How The Stats Are Determined

Future stats are determined by a variety of factors, some of which are:

  • Player's Age (Are they entering a prime? Leaving? In it?)
  • Current state of their team (Is their team losing? The more they are, the more that player gets to shine
  • Future state of their team (Any free agent moves? Big draft picks?)
  • Position (Some get more possession than others)
None of this should be taken seriously of course, as it is impossible to guarantee what a player will do in the future.
